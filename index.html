Privacy Policy

1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Moto-Rishta! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app and services.

By using Moto-Rishta, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services.

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Address
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Blood group and emergency contact (for safety during rides)

2.2 Location Data

We collect real-time location data only while the app is in use, and only when participating in rides.

Location data is used for safety monitoring, ride coordination, and tracking.

Your real-time location will be shared as follows:

  • With ride administrators for safety purposes
  • If you are a ride administrator, your location will be shared with fellow riders for better coordination and group management

We do not collect background location data when the app is closed or not in use.

2.3 Ride & Motorcycle Information

  • Users may voluntarily enter motorcycle details such as model and maintenance history
  • This data is used for maintenance reminders and personal record-keeping

2.4 User Content

  • Users may create posts, articles, group rides, and events
  • Users control whether their content is public or private within the app

2.5 Payment & Subscription Data

Currently, Moto-Rishta does not offer in-app purchases or subscriptions. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. All future transactions, if any, will be processed securely via Google Play or Apple App Store.

2.6 Device Information

We collect device information for app improvement and debugging purposes.

3. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Secure access controls
  • Regular security reviews and audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage users to protect their account credentials.

4. DATA RETENTION & DELETION

4.1 Retention Policy

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve user experience

4.2 Deleting Your Account

Users can request account deletion by:

  1. Navigating to Settings > Delete Account in the app
  2. Sending a request to our support team at info@raad-m.com

Upon deletion, all personal data will be permanently removed, except where required by law.

5. YOUR RIGHTS & CHOICES

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal data

For any of these requests, contact us at info@raad-m.com.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Moto-Rishta is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us for immediate removal.

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and significant updates will be communicated through the app or via email.

8. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: info@raad-m.com

Website: https://raad-m.com
